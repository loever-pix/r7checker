Add Account

Connect your Ubisoft account to view your R6 Siege collection. Sign in with your email & password and we'll fetch it for you — or, if you'd rather not share your password, paste a raw ticket / run a 1-line command to get one yourself.

1 Sign in with your Ubisoft account

Enter your Ubisoft email and password. We log in on your behalf through a rotating residential proxy and load your locker. Tip: you can paste a email:password combo straight into the email box — we'll split it for you.

Your credentials are only used to fetch your data and are never stored.

1 Paste the JSON Ubisoft returned

If you've logged in to Ubisoft's API in your terminal or DevTools, paste the entire JSON response below. The fields we need: ticket, userId, sessionId, and ideally rememberMeTicket.

You can also paste just the ticket value alone — but we need at least ticket + userId to load your data.

2 Submit — server validates + persists

We hit /v3/users/me on Ubisoft with your ticket to verify it. If valid, the ticket + rememberMeTicket are stored (encrypted on disk) and used to fetch your inventory + seasons. Future visits use the rememberMeTicket to auto-refresh — no re-login needed for ~30 days.
